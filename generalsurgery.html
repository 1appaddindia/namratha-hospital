GENERAL SURGERY

Our hospital offers a comprehensive range of general surgical services aimed at diagnosing and treating a variety of medical conditions. We specialize in providing personalized care and advanced surgical techniques to address abdominal, gastrointestinal, and other surgical needs.

At Namratha Hospital, our team of skilled general surgeons is committed to delivering high-quality care using state-of-the-art technology and evidence-based practices. We perform a wide array of surgical procedures, including appendectomies, gallbladder surgeries, hernia repairs, and gastrointestinal surgeries. Our approach emphasizes minimally invasive techniques whenever possible, ensuring faster recovery times, reduced postoperative discomfort, and improved outcomes for our patients.

We prioritize patient safety, comfort, and well-being throughout their surgical experience. From pre-operative consultations to post-operative care and follow-up, Namratha Hospital provides compassionate support and comprehensive treatment plans tailored to meet the individual needs of each patient. Whether for elective procedures or urgent surgical interventions, you can trust Namratha Hospital for excellence in general surgery and dedicated patient care.

Our Core Values

Namratha Hospital is renowned for its personalized patient care across a range of medical specialties. Today, the hospital is recognized as a world-renowned institution, known not only for providing outstanding care and treatment but also for improving outcomes through comprehensive medical research. Our hospital has touched the lives of millions, offering care and treatment to the sickest in our community, including rehabilitation and aged care.
